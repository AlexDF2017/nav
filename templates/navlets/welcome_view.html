Thanks for using NAV!

The widgets on this page provide information and shortcuts to NAV's tools.

You can add a widget by clicking the "Add widget" button on the top right of the page, then choose one from the list that appears.

If you are not happy with the placement, you can move them by clicking the "Reorder widgets" button and dragging them around.

Some widgets have an "Edit" button next to the "Remove" button. Click this to see and adjust the preferences for the widget. The preferences are personal and lets you decide how the widget should work.

To remove a widget, just click the "Remove" button. If you have read this info, then removing this widget will make room for other, more interesting ones!
