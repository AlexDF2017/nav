Thanks for using NAV!

The widgets on this page are called "Navlets", and provide information and shortcuts to NAV's tools.

You can add a Navlet by clicking the "Add Navlet" button on the top right of the page, then choose one from the list that appears.

If you are not happy with the placement, you can move them by clicking the "Sort Navlets" button and dragging them around.

Some Navlets have an "Edit" button next to the "Remove" button. Click this to see and adjust the preferences for the Navlet. The preferences are personal and lets you decide how the Navlet should work.

To remove a Navlet, just click the "Remove" button. If you have read this info, then removing this Navlet will make room for other, more interesting Navlets!
